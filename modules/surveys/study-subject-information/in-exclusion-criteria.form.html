Inclusion and exclusion criteria
Remove
Inclusion criteria
Age is between 25 and 65 years
Current driver's license
Are you able to speak and write in English?
Is it likely that the participant can perform neurobehavioral tests?
Exclusion criteria
Are you a night-shift worker (i.e. between 1 am and 6 am)?
Are you an inexperienced driver? (< 2 hrs/week and < 2 years).
Are you currently pregnant or breastfeeding (female) or do you care for a new-born?
Have you participated in another clinical research study trial in the last 12 months?
Do you smoke?
Have been taking any recreational drugs in the last 60 days?
Do you drink alcohol?
Have you travelled overseas in the past 2 months?
Do you have any allergies?
Have you ever been diagnosed with a sleep condition in the past?
Have you used a CPAP or mandibular advancement device, or did you undergo CBT-I or take sleep medication?
Have you ever been diagnosed with: (please tick each box that applies, and exclude if severe).
Have you previously had a stroke or head injury?
Do you have any other physical health conditions for which you have recently received medical treatment?
Have you been diagnosed with any psychiatric disorder such as anxiety, depression, mania or psychosis?
Do you have any other medical conditions or have you been in hospital recently?
Are you taking any medications? This includes antidepressants and any medication to assist your sleep as well as over the counter medications and supplements such as vitamins and minerals.
